With One2Many, we wanted to change the my photos into our photos, using a group collaboration app involving all your friends and family. It allows everyone in the group to select certain content from each other’s different points of view and stitch them into a video. The application allows every member participating to be featured in the video and encourages them to input their own content. The app eliminates many additional steps for those who wish to travel lightly and think a phone alone is sufficient. Developed natively with Swift and Java, One2many is a mobile app available on both the Google Play Store and App Store.
